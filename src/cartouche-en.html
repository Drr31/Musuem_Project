Hieroglyphic cartridge

Artwork: Royal Cartridge

Date of discovery: 1821

Place: Siwa

Dimensions: 75cm x 44cm

Weight: 176 grams

Exhibition space in the museum: Egyptian Halle

It was in 1821 when Champollion discovered the first royal cartridges. He also deciphered Cleopatra's cartridge. A cartridge is a hieroglyphic symbol that contains the name of a pharaoh. There are five cartridges for five pharaohs.

Gthanks to Champollion, the deciphering of the cartridges has gradually developed. This cartridge extract shows symbols each belonging to a letter of Cleopatra. The lion lying on the right side of the object belongs, for example, to Cleopatra's "l".
