Pedagogical visits

Cultural visits

Jean-François Champollion and Egypte

In this visit we will discuss various themes such as Champollion's deciphering of the hieroglyphs, the personality and processes of the researcher and his passion for Egypt. But especially the ancient Egyptian gods and religious beliefs at the time of the pharaohs.

The birth of the writing

We will show you pictograms, ideograms and phonograms. Explore the founding writings : cuneiform, hieroglyphics, Asian and Mayan writings. This by understanding the challenges of writing and the nature then the tracing of the first writings invented by humankind.

The book, the letter and the citizen

f you're wondering how a book is made or even what a book is, this is the time to dig deeper into your culture. Learn about the history of the letters of our alphabet and the discovery of paper from the Middle Ages to the present day.

Entertainment visits

Visits for everyone

The Scribes' Assembly

Jean-François Champollion invites scribes to dinner in his house. The children must go to the halls of the museum to acquire these scribes while learning the writings they will be practicing too .

The Prince with the broken nose

Prince Achur-Malik's nose was broken in the Museum. A crime that the children have to solve by looking for clues such as the murder weapon in the museum's artwork to ultimately find out who did it. (Playful Activity)

The Mystery of the Mummy

Curse, a mummy is cursed and unable to reach his kingdom. The goal is that the children must understand why and must help him to reach the Afterlife. They will have to study the mummy and the funeral rituals.

travel log of Champollion Museum

With their travel log in hand, the children walk around the museum and answer questions that allow them to take an interest in the artworks in the collections.

Pedagogical workshops

Cultural workshops

The Old Egypt

A workshop to learn more about Egyptian civilization in order to understand the range of the higher forces and the symbolic figures of the pantheon in old Egypt.

The Scriptures of the World

Encoding and decoding scriptures and hieroglyphics, you'll know everything. Children will have to understand the characters and will use their imagination. Initiation to hieroglyphics is the key goal of this activity.
