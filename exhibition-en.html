Steno Stone Exhibition - Michel Danton

Egypt Exhibition, First Impressions

The Champollion museum proposes you an exhibition of the works of the artist Michel Danton. Dive into the exploration of stenography this winter from December 21st to January 29th 2019-2020. Stenography is an abbreviated form of writing formed by conventional signs.

This exhibition shows the first photographs taken in Egypt in the years 1850-1880. The photographs illustrate the archaeological sites who have been discovered by the first archaeologists.
The photographs will be supplemented by objects from excavations from that era.

Easter Island Exhibition - The Talking Woods

Easter Island is one of the last lands settled by man around the 10th century. Scientific achievements and the evolution of this archipelago will fascinate you as much as the presentation of the kohau rongorongo tablets during the exhibition.
