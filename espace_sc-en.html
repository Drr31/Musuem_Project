The documentation center

The museum's documentation centre is located in the conservation building at 9 street of Colomb. It gathers books and reviews on the themes of the collections: Champollion, Egypt, the history of world writings, the history of books...
The museum is open Monday to Friday from 8:30 to 12:30 and from 14:00 to 17:30.
The documentation centre is accessible to all by appointment, Monday to Friday.
